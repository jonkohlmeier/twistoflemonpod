About the Podcast

Life with a Twist of Lemon is a podcast where Jon Kohlmeier and Stan Lemon discuss technology, finance, life, craftsmanship, theology, and everything else with a twist of lemon.

The Hosts

Jon Kohlmeier

Jon brings his unique perspective on technology, craftsmanship, and life to every episode.
